## Using KDE builtin audio setting
1. open status and notifications by clicking more icon (^) in system tray
2. click on right-up-corner 'Configure system tray'
3. select entries tab and add keyboard shortcut for audio volume (ctrl+alt+a)
4. a. press ctrl+alt+a and use arrow key, tab, shift+tab, enter to switch   
b. press ctrl+alt+a and use mouse click to switch desired sinks
